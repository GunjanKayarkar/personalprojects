We are an American-based chain of fast food restaurants originating in Irvine, California in 1962, by founder Glen Bell. Our restaurants serve a variety of Mexican-inspired foods, that include: tacos, burritos, quesadillas, nachos, novelty and specialty items, along with a variety of "value menu" items.

We believe that our customers deserve the very best. So in our quest to do that we serve each one of our customers the best food made with the best of the ingredients available out there. Never compromising on the quality as well as the taste of our dishes and satisfying our customers is our number 1 choice and we live by it.

Along with our open kitchen design and our California inspired contemporary decor, you'll find a mix of seating areas, music and lighting to make you feel at home.
We have a maniacal focus on our customers and we always celebrate the accomplishments of our people. Sounds like you? Give any of our nearby restaurants a visit and find out why our chain of restaurants are among some of the best in the world.
